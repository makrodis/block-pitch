About Block, Inc. (Company Description)

Block (formerly Square) is a financial technology company focusing on digital payments and financial services through two main ecosystems: Square for businesses and Cash App for consumers. (Company Introduction)

Square Ecosystem (Business Products)

  • Payment Processing (Card Processing Service)
  • Point of Sale Systems (Hardware Solutions)
  • Business Banking (Financial Services)
  • Square Capital (Business Loans)

Cash App (Consumer Products)

  • P2P Payments (Person-to-Person Transfers)
  • Stock Investing (Retail Investment Platform)
  • Bitcoin Trading (Cryptocurrency Service)
  • Banking Services (Consumer Banking)
